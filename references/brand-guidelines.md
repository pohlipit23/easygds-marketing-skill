# easyGDS Brand Guidelines

## Brand Positioning
easyGDS is the travel ecosystem for your brand. We provide airlines, travel agents, and loyalty & retail with the complete ecosystem for travel commerce: Technology, Product and Inventory, Service and Expertise.

**Category:** Travel Commerce Platform
**Tagline:** Travel Commerce. Simplified.

## Target Audiences

### Airlines
- Platform, not individual tools
- Own and keep your direct customer relationship
- Revenue growth through packaging and ancillaries
- Power your airline holiday brand
- Systems that scale

### Travel Agents
- Your B2B and B2C platform
- Access to vast and competitive global inventory
- Transparent cost and pricing
- Integrate your existing content
- Pay only for what you use

### Loyalty & Retail
- Launch your travel vertical without adding complexity
- Products your customers will love
- Maintain 1st party customer ownership
- Global inventory
- Earn & Redeem partnership

## Tone of Voice

**We are:**
- Calm
- Direct
- Problem solving
- Precise

**Writing Rules:**
- Short sentences
- Active voice
- No buzzwords
- Problem solving orientated

## Good vs Bad Examples

✓ "easyGDS gives travel businesses full control over distribution and pricing."
✗ "Unlock next generation travel innovation!"

**Rule:** If a sentence sounds impressive but says nothing, remove it.

## Guardrails

**Never:**
- Use hyperbolic language
- Lead with trends or buzzwords
- Overcomplicate the message

**Always:**
- Prioritise clarity over cleverness
- Speak from within the industry, not as marketers
- Keep pricing and value transparent

## Value Proposition
A single platform that allows airlines, travel agents and loyalty & retail businesses to manage distribution, pricing, packaging and servicing customers without cost or complexity.

**What we do:**
- Transparent pricing
- Technology built to scale
- Product & Inventory competitive and quality

**What we don't do:**
- Hidden costs
- Just for show products
- Things we don't believe in
