# easyGDS Product Portfolio

## Platform Overview

easyGDS brings every part of airline commerce together. Flights, hotels, tours, ancillaries and loyalty in one airline branded ecosystem. Complete flexibility to combine products in dynamic packages, or fixed tour packages. Integrate your existing content and combine with Tripovo products and inventory for ultimate choics. Control the customer journey end-to-end and unlock new revenue streams without adding complexity.

## Product Architecture

### B2C Platform
- Mobile, Desktop, AI chat in a white-label SAAS platform
- Make it your brand: logos, colors, booking flows, emails
- Alternatively use the easyGDS-API to build your own UX and control every part of the experience
- No coding needed: drag, drop, launch in hours

### B2B Platform
- Contact Centre, POS outlets, 3rd party agencies and other distribution partners
- Manage a multi-hieracy network of sub-agencies and outlets
- Custom commission structures and/or net rate distribution
- Manage deposits and credit lines

### easyGDS Travel Products
- Dynamic Packages
- Fixed Tour Packages
- Stopover Packages
- Single products: Flight Only, Hotel Only
- Car Rental
- Transfers
- Tours/Activities
- Air Ancillaries
- Branded Fares and Fare Families

### Payment Platform
- 80+ payment channels
- 50+ currencies
- PCI Level 1 security
- Cards, wallets, local payment methods
- Partners: Stripe, Adyen, Barclaycard, Cybersource, PayFort, 2C2P, and more

### Loyalty Platform
- Open connector to airline loyalty and membership platforms
- Points earn & burn
- Mixed form of payment: cash & points
- Dynamic redemption and accrual through integration with membership platforms

## Key Features

### Dynamic Packages
Empower customers to build dynamic packages in a single flow. Flights + Hotels + Experiences in a complete holiday shopping basket.

### Standalone Products
Sell standalone hotels, car rental and activities to flight-only bookers. Keep the journey under your brand.

### Automated Cross Sell
- Promote hotels, transfers, tours & activities via post-booking and pre-trip communication
- Automatic PNR data retrieval (destination, dates, pax count, currency)
- Easy integration through Google Tag Manager

### Stopover Experiences
- Standalone Stopover Booking Engine OR Integrated into the airline's flight booking engine
- Multiple inventories to build complete stopover experiences (transfers, tours, hotels, tickets)
- B2B to distribute through your ticket offices, contact center and 3rd party agents and distribution partners
- Example: Etihad Stopover powered by easyGDS

### Merchandising & Analytics
- Pre-built templates for email and social marketing
- Dynamic up-sell prompts in booking flow
- Promocode / Evoucher management for granular and targetted sales promotions
- Real-time tracking of package sales and ancillary cross-sell
- Adobe and Google Analytics integrations
- Data feed into your data warehouse
